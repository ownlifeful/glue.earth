Dear People,


GLUE is a grassroots movement that empowers workers of all backgrounds to come together and fight for their rights to fair wages, safe working conditions, and dignified treatment. We believe that all workers, regardless of their race, gender, sexual orientation, or political affiliation, deserve to be treated with respect and dignity. We don't ask for your personal information or money - we simply ask for your support in spreading awareness and taking action to create a world where every worker is valued and able to live with dignity. Join us and stand up against worker exploitation and oppression today.
